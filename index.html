About the Artist

Agun Bera

They say you can’t be a "gym boy" and a "cozy soul" at the same time, but I’ve always found that the hardest workouts lead to the most peaceful art. My name is Agni, and I am a multidisciplinary artist dedicated to the beauty of the "calm." From the precision of a drawing to the fleeting magic of a photograph, my goal is to share the emotional depth I find in everyday life. Whether I’m strumming my ukulele or working on a new Taba piece, I strive to create a space where people can breathe, feel, and simply be.

My work spans landscape, portrait, and documentary photography, always anchored to natural light and the rawness of unposed moments. I don't stage. I wait.
